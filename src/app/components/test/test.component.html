At the core of Spotly, community is everything. Eliminating the headaches of parking for the general public is just one of the missions for Spotly. Empowering community members by becoming Spotly Hosts on this new platform is both economically sensible and environmentally conscious. In doing this, Spotly will drastically reduce the amount of land that's destroyed to build more unnecessary parking lots, cut the time wasted driving aimlessly searching for a parking spot and provide side income to the Hosts who need it.
